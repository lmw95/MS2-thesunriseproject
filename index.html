Lowestoft

Once a busy fishing town, Lowestoft holds an abundance of maritime history and culture connected to the sea

  • Britain's most easterly town
  • Oldest settled place in Britain - tools found and dated at 700,000 years old!
  • Maritime & fishing history landmarks include the Scores and the Harbour
